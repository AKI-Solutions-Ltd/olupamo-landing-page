Terms and Conditions For The Money Back Guarantee

Refund Policy

At Olupamo Accounting Solutions, we are committed to providing high-quality financial management software to schools. We aim to convey our confidence in our accounting solution to our customers. By offering a refund, we demonstrate our willingness to go the extra mile to ensure customer satisfaction. Our return policy is valid for 30 days. If 30 days have passed since your subscription, we are unable to offer a refund.

Eligibility

Our 30 days money back guarantee applies to all schools that purchase our accounting software solution. To be eligible for refund, the school must meet the following condition:

  • The school must have subscribe to our software solution directly from our website or through our platform
  • The school must have paid the purchase price for our software
  • The school must request s refund within 30 days of the date of subscription
  • The school must have use our software in accordance with our instructions and guidelines

Refunds

If you would like to request a refund, please send an email to info@eschool.ng with your name and email address associated with your account, and a detailed reason for requesting a refund. Once your refund mail is received, we will send an email to notify you that we have received your refund request. Then our customer support team will review the request and may need additional information or support logs to verify the issue. If approved, the refund will be processed within 3 days of receiving the request.

Refund amount

The refund amount will be the full purchase price paid by the school, minus any applicable taxes or fees. If your refund has been approved by our team, and you haven’t received your refund within the expected timeline, please check your bank account to ensure the refund wasn’t deposited, also verify the accounting information is accurate and up to date, you can also contact our support team at info@eschool.ng for assistance. We will investigate and work with our payment service provider to resolve the issue and ensure you receive your refund as soon as possible.

Exceptions

Our 30 day money back guarantee does not apply to the following:

  • Software account that have been used for illegal or unauthorized purposes
  • Software subscriptions that have been purchased through an unauthorized reseller or third party.

We reserve the right to modify or update our refund policy at any time without notice. The current refund policy will always be available on our website.
